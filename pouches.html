What NOT to Buy

  • Compression cubes (general) — community consensus: don't compress enough on daily-wear clothes to justify bulk
  • Bagail / Amazon Basics no-name cubes — zippers fail, sausage shape after 6 months
  • Peak Design Wash Pouch — already have toiletry kit
  • Bellroy Standing Pouch / Plus — discontinued. Use Cinch Pouch instead.
  • Evergoods CIVIC Access Pouch 2L at ~€95+ — CAP 1L is better value at $75 (~€90 puerta) but still expensive vs Aer/Tom Bihn
  • Pills pouch / Bellroy Pencil Case — don't carry meds daily
  • Document/passport pouch — Recon hidden secret pocket is enough
  • Thule Compression Cube Set — no black colorway available
