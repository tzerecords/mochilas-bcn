What NOT to Buy

  • Compression cubes (general) — community consensus: don't actually compress enough on daily-wear clothes to justify the bulk
  • Bagail / Amazon Basics no-name cubes — zippers fail, sausage shape after 6 months
  • Peak Design Wash Pouch — you already have a toiletry kit
  • Bellroy Tech Kit (regular non-Compact) at €89 — overpriced vs alternatives
  • Evergoods CIVIC Access Pouch 2L (€95+) — Tom Bihn or Aer Slim are equivalent at half cost
  • Pills pouch / Bellroy Pencil Case — you don't carry meds daily
  • Document/passport pouch — you don't travel internationally enough; secret pocket of Recon is enough
